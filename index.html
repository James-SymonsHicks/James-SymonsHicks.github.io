About Me

Pre-Doctoral Research Assistant (LSE)

I am a Pre-Doctoral Research Assistant at the LSE Centre for Macroeconomics, focusing on Fiscal Policy, Productivity & Growth, and Family Macroeconomics.

I have also worked as a Research Assistant at University College London (UCL) with a focus on financial decision-making and experimental economics.

I completed my MSc Economics at UCL in September 2024 and my BSc Economics at UCL in 2023.

My research interests include:

  • Financial markets
  • Financial decision-making
  • Macroeconomics
